THREE APPROACHES

Methodology

01

Physics Simulation

Inserts a synthetic titanium-density ellipse at a randomised position within the petrous temporal bone, then ray-casts outward to deposit exponentially-decaying bright/dark beam-hardening streaks. Gaussian photon-starvation noise is added as a final pass.

No training Deterministic < 2 s
02

Dictionary Learning

A 128-atom dictionary was trained offline on overlapping 16 × 16 pixel patches from real cochlear-implant CT slices using MiniBatchDictionaryLearning. At inference, each patch of the phantom is sparse-coded against the atoms (OMP, k = 6) and back-projected to reconstruct a texture-consistent image.

Pre-trained atoms SSIM-scored ~5–10 s
03

LoRA Diffusion

Stable Diffusion v1.5 was fine-tuned for 1 500 steps on z-filtered axial CT slices using Low-Rank Adaptation (rank 16, α 32) applied to the UNet's attention projections. The web app runs img2img inference: the skull phantom seeds the latent, and the prompt steers the artifact characteristics.

LoRA rank 16 SD v1.5 ~30–90 s
